A Little About Me

I was born with beautiful and bouncy curly hair, though for years I hated and neglected it. Not knowing what to do with the curly frizz ball on my head, I brushed it out, making it worse(! At the end of seventh grade, I came upon a video of a girl whos hair looked just like mine! I clicked on the video out of interest and found out that she had transformed her damaged poofy hair into luscious ringlets. I got soo excited and immediately started watching all of her videos to learn what she had done to achieve her gorgeous hair.
